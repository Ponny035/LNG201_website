Background & Processes

YouTube is a famous video platform where people can watch, upload or comment on videos. This website is mainly known for its entertainments videos. However, several video artists (youtubers) want to bring more than just entertainment and prefer to share their knowledge with us. This is another way to learn or help understand a topic. On top of that, YouTube is easily accessible for everyone and the learning by video can be fun. we choose this because YouTube has an important place in our lives. Indeed, we use it every day but in different ways. Some of us, use it to listen music, to laugh, to be informed about the news or even to learn. Therefore, we are curious to know how people use YouTube as a learning platform.

Interview Case Studies

Our group interviewed three people consist of one Ph.D student and two alumni. As a group, we created four open-ended questions about “YouTube as an online learning platform” We wanted to know how much YouTube's video effect to them and their perspectives about online learning platform. All interviews were arranged well in advance and flexibility of interview times was essential. Each interview was recorded to be used on our group’s website. We asked permission from all interviewees if that was acceptable to them. They all answered positively.


This are four open-ended questions that we used.
  1. Why do you an online learning platform as your learning resource?
  2. Do you think that videos in YouTube are realiable or not. Why?
  3. Do you think that Youtube can be an online learning platform. Why?
  4. What is your opinion if I say that "YouTube is more effective than class room in term of learning"?

First, Mr.Amontep wijitcharoen (P'Bird) who is a Ph.D student in Computer Science that use a lot of online learning platform to study. In first question he said that when he was a child when he want to learn he need to go to library and you need to wait util it open but nowdays he can learn in any time that he want. Second question he said that their is a plenty of content has been put on YouTube every day it depend on the chanel that you study his or her profile and comment is their any agrument and compare this information with other source. Next question he think it depend on the content and YouTubers for genaral content you can use it but in deep down you need to go to other platform. Last, question he think it more effective but it also depend on each person because in each person have their own way to learn.



Second, Mr.Pittawat Taveekitworachai (Pete) Who is a SIT alumni that good at web-devlopment by study from online. In the first question he use online learning platform because it very easy and flexible he can learn any content in time from any where. Second question he said that before he watch a video he will research how realiable of this YouTuber is a video have any support evidence or not. Next question he think that Youtube can be an online learning platform but not comfortable as a real online learning platform because real online learning platform their have feture that can help you learn like test and instructor is more concentrate to their student. In last question he belive that class is more benifit than YouTube like you can ask instructor directly when you don't understand.



Last, Mr.Chaichet Phaibunwitthayasak (Praem) Who is a SIT alumni that use online learning platform to study when he don't understand topic in class. In first question Praem said that he use online learning platform because it easy to access and most of videos is free to learn. In Second he think that many of videos are realiable if it come from realiable user and he consider in by number of subscriber. In next question he think that YouTube can be an online learning because YouTube consist of many type of videos and in the last question he think in some part YouTube can be effective than classroom like you learn from any where on Earth.
